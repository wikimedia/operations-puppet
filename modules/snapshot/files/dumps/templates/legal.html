License information

Wikimedia’s mission is to create educational content that is freely available to all people. In keeping with that goal, all information on Wikimedia projects may be freely shared, copied, remixed, and used for any purpose (including commercial purposes!) in perpetuity. To help guide users of dumps.wikimedia.org, this page contains more detailed information about Wikimedia’s licensing and licensing policies as they may apply to our dumps.

This is a high-level guide only. Where this information conflicts with specific information in the Wikimedia Foundation Terms of Use, or with other information contained inside the dumps themselves, this description should be ignored. Those terms are controlling.

Images

By default, images uploaded to our services are under the Creative Commons Attribution-Share-Alike 3.0 License. However, many images are NOT released under Creative Commons. Image copyright information is contained in the image description page inside the text dumps.
